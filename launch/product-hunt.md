## Short Description (260 char max)


## Full Description

```
Invisible AI transcription is the fastest-growing privacy threat in remote work.

Tools like Granola ($1.5B valuation), Otter.ai (facing a class-action lawsuit), and Fireflies.ai can silently capture your meeting audio — no recording indicator, no consent prompt, no way for you to know.

Nullify fixes this.

🔍 DETECT
Real-time process and network monitoring detects 8+ transcription tools the moment they activate. Works across Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and any other platform.

🛡️ PROTECT
Audio Shield uses psychoacoustic perturbation to make AI transcription produce garbled, unusable text — while your voice sounds perfectly normal to human participants.

⚡ KEY FEATURES
• Detects Granola, Otter.ai, Fireflies, Read.ai, tl;dv, Fathom, Supernormal, Tactiq
• 4 protection levels (Stealth → Maximum)
• 100% local processing — no data leaves your computer
• Free and open source (MIT license)
• macOS and Windows

In 13 US states, recording without consent is illegal. Under GDPR, it violates data protection laws. Regardless of jurisdiction — you deserve to know when you're being recorded.
```

## Maker Comment (primo commento)

```
Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I built Nullify because I discovered that a colleague was using Granola to silently transcribe all our team meetings — without telling anyone. No recording indicator, no consent, nothing. Our 1-on-1s, salary discussions, and candid team conversations were all being captured and stored on third-party servers.

When I looked into it, I realized this is a massive and growing problem:
- Granola just raised $125M at a $1.5B valuation
- Otter.ai is facing a class-action lawsuit for recording without consent
- Stanford has banned AI meeting bots entirely

The worst part? Traditional platform indicators (like Zoom's recording dot) don't detect any of these tools. They operate at the system audio level, completely invisible.

So I built Nullify. It monitors for process-level and network-level signatures of these tools and alerts you instantly. And if you can't stop the recording, Audio Shield uses psychoacoustic techniques to make the AI transcript unusable — while your voice sounds perfectly normal to everyone on the call.

It's completely free, open source (MIT), and 100% local — no data ever leaves your machine.

I'd love your feedback. What features would you want to see next?

GitHub: https://github.com/khaoss85/nullify
```
